Step 1

Visit an Aadhaar enrolment center

Aadhaar enrolment is an agency appointed for collecting demographic and biometric information of individuals, and includes certain Banks and Post Offices which have been designated as Aadhaar Enrolment Centres.

  1. Locate the Enrolment Centre nearest to you
  2. Book an appointment

Step 2

Fill the Aadhaar enrolment form

At the Centre, you will be required to fill in the Aadhaar Enrolment Form

Step 3

Submit one valid proof of identity and address

You are also required to submit the originals of one valid Proof of Identity (PoI) document and one valid Proof of Address (PoA) document.

  1. List of acceptable items
I used my voters ID and my passport as proof of identity and address respectively.

Step 4

Your biometric data will be collected

Your biometric data shall also be taken as part of the enrolment. It includes your

Photographs
Fingerprints
Iris scan

Step 5

Get an enrolment slip

You will get an acknowledgement slip with an Enrolment Number and other details you have given during enrolment.

This is how the enrollment slip looks.

Step 6

Wait for confirmation

Your Aadhaar will be ready in 60-90 days. Once the Aadhaar is ready, you receive an SMS on the registered mobile number.

  1. You can check if your Aadhaar is ready using your Enrolment ID here.

  2. Verify whether your Aadhaar is genuine or not here.

Step 7

Delivery

After Aadhaar is ready, your Aadhaar Card with your Aadhaar number and other details are enclosed in a physical letter which will be delivered to you.
